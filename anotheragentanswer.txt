## 💎 The SaaS Philosophy: Workspaces over Sparse Links

By choosing a workspace-focused layout, Verynt delivers several significant advantages:
1. **Fluid Workflows:** Instead of constantly loading different single-page sites, users carry out complex processes inside a single unified dashboard (e.g., uploading a raw audio file inside *Whisper*, transcribing it, and then instantly transferring that text into *DocuChat* for summarizing).
2. **Infinite Scale & Zero Server Cost:** 100% of processing operates client-side via IndexedDB, Web Audio APIs, Canvas contexts, and on-device WebGPU execution.
3. **Blistering Compilation Speed:** Code-splitting routes via Vite ensuring that each complex tool (ranging from Whisper transcription to 3D graph renders) loads lazily, optimizing overall site speed and organic SEO visibility.
